The Story of Piper Princess of Prussia

Piper Princess of Prussia is the beautiful child of Icculus and Tela. She is kind, sweet, but chaotic.

How did she become Princess of the Land of Lizards you ask? She defeated the Evil King Wilson with the power of the Helping Friendly Book.

Some of Piper's Powers include:

  • Flawless Fur to protect her from the Evil King Wilson's magic
  • Fierce and ferocious bark that wards off evil
  • Friendly and charming to those who merit kindness
  • Faithful loyalty to the Lizard People
